Latitude vs. Temperature

As expected, the weather become significantly warmer as one approaches the equator (0 Deg. Lattitude). You can also see that there are less datapoints as the lattitude gets lower, this is due to the fact that there are no landmasses after -60 besides at the south pole. More interestingly, however, is the fact that the souther hemisphere tends to be warmer this time of year than the northern hemisphere. This may be due to the tilt of the earth at the time of year this data was gathered
